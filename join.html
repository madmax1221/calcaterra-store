A QUIET CORRESPONDENCE

No club. No tier.
A short list.

Calcaterra began with a single conviction: that an object made deliberately can outlast the moment it was made in. There is nothing to subscribe to in the usual sense. No membership card. No fee. No marketing schedule.

But there is a list. A quiet correspondence with those who decided to be here early, before the rest of the world knew the house existed. Infrequent. Considered. Never sold to anyone.

Three good reasons

What it means.

I.

Early access.

References reach you before they reach the site. New colorways, new pieces, the first to know. By the time anyone else has heard, you have already decided.

II.

The correspondence.

Atelier notes, editorial moments, and considered releases, sent only when there is something worth saying. No campaigns. No frequency targets. Nothing that fills your inbox.

III.

A permanent record.

An account links your timepieces to your name in our records. Your warranty, your activation codes, your ownership, kept against the day they matter. Optional, recommended, never required.

Two ways to stay close

Choose the one that fits.

The list

Join the correspondence.

An email address is all we ask. We write infrequently, never sell the list, and you can leave at any time.

The record

Open an account.

The full membership. Watch registration, warranty management, order history, and ownership records kept in your name, permanently.
