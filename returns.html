Returns Policy.

We stand behind every timepiece we make. If something isn't right, we will make it right.

THE POLICY

14 days. Conditions apply.

Calcaterra offers a 14-day return window from the date of delivery. Returns are accepted where the following conditions are met:

  • The timepiece is unworn and in original condition
  • All original packaging, documentation, and accessories are included
  • A return authorisation has been obtained from our team prior to dispatch
  • The return is initiated within 14 days of the delivery date
RETURN WINDOW 14 days from delivery date
CONDITION REQUIRED Unworn, original packaging, all accessories included
REFUND SCOPE Product price only. Original shipping costs are non-refundable.
REFUND METHOD Original payment method, within 5–10 business days after inspection and approval
RETURN SHIPPING Customer's responsibility unless item is faulty or incorrect
EXCHANGES Available for different variants subject to stock availability
HOW TO RETURN

Three steps.

01

Contact us first

Email orders@calcaterra.co with your order reference and reason for return. We will respond within 24 hours with a return authorisation.

02

Pack it carefully

Repack the watch in its original Calcaterra box with all included accessories and documentation. Use the outer packaging or equivalent protective packaging for transit.

03

Ship it back

Send the parcel using a tracked and insured shipping service. Calcaterra cannot be held responsible for items lost or damaged in transit. Share the tracking number with us once dispatched.

IMPORTANT
Returns without prior
authorisation will not be accepted.

Always contact us before dispatching your return. Unauthorised returns may be refused on arrival.

EXCEPTIONS

What we cannot accept.

Returns will not be accepted for items that show signs of wear, have been altered or resized, are missing components, are returned outside the 14-day window, or are dispatched without prior authorisation. Such returns may be refused on arrival and sent back at the customer's cost.

If you are uncertain whether your return qualifies, contact us at orders@calcaterra.co before dispatching.
